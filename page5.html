The bedroom looks normal on the outside, but once you take a closer look, things start to feel weird. The bed is a typical queen-sized mattress with black sheets and a Batman themed comforter, but that doesn’t make sense, he loved Superman not Bat- ‘he’, wait, who is ‘he’? Who were you just talking about? You don’t know.

The room itself is filled with knickknacks, superhero themed knickknacks, ranging from DC’s Green Lantern to Marvel’s Fantastic Four. There were pop figures, comic books, posters, life-sized figurines, replicas, and so much more, but something was off. You were sure of it. Once again you felt that the displays were too perfect, as if they were attempting to convince you of something, something that you were still unaware of yourself.

That’s when you see it. A picture on the dresser that you didn’t notice before. A picture of you smiling along with… the man… the man dead on the floor. But that can’t be right, ‘he’ didn’t look like- WHO IS ‘HE’??
